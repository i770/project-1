UGALI

Ugali is a staple food in many African countries, particularly in East Africa, including Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and parts of Southern and Central Africa. It is a type of maize flour porridge, similar to polenta or grits, and is known by different names in various regions, such as sadza in Zimbabwe, nsima in Malawi, pap in South Africa, and posho in Uganda.
